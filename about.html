About

Dakota Bragato is a freelance journalist and data specialist with experience working across the US and UK. She specializes in public health and cultural reporting, with a particular focus in reproductive justice. She has reported for Raven News and has done editorial work for Street Sense Media. Before moving into journalism, she spent several years in perinatal and early childhood health policy research, and has also worked in nonprofit communications, community organizing, and arts and tech education. She holds a BA in American Studies from Cornell University, and an MSc in Digital Journalism from Goldsmiths, University of London.
